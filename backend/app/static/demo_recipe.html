Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between two slices, butter side facing out.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook sandwich 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  5. Slice and serve hot.
